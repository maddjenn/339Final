About Me

Madeleine Jennings
I like to make things; and break them, ML Research and Predictive Medicine. I am an incoming product designer @ Xometry and currently studying UX Design, biopsychology, cognition and neuroscience with a minor in computer science at the University of Michigan. I am the VP of marketing and design @ KTP, intermediate instructor @ GEECs, disambiguation research assistant @ UMSI and painter @ digipant. Outside of design I love reading Philosophy and Sci-fi books, drawing with charcoal, and treasure hunting. Some of my favorite TV shows are La Casa de Papel, Fleabag and Gravity Falls. I am really interested in robotics and medicine and how design can tie into these two concepts to make it easier to use and more accessible hope in the future I can create medical devices that will better the quality of life for people and maybe even make Baymax from big hero 6 with predictive medicine technologies implemented.
